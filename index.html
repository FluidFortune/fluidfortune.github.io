[ ABOUT FLUID FORTUNE ]

Fluid Fortune is an independent forge dedicated to building mindful, useful tech. We understand the importance of large systems while also seeing their inner ironies. We operate under a core philosophy: the internet should be a resource, not a dependency, and one should never take it too seriously.

We build tools for the era of private intelligence. You connect to the network to download the payload, but if you choose, the compute itself can stay entirely on your machine. Living by the subscription model should be a choice, not a requirement.

THE PHANTOM

v0.1.0-alpha
